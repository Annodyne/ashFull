Phase 1/2 Study of INCB039110 (JAK1 Inhibitor) in Combination With Ibrutinib in Subjects
With Relapsed or Refractory Diffuse Large B-cell Lymphoma


STUDY OBJECTIVES


STUDY DESIGN


KEY INCLUSION &
EXCLUSION CRITERIA




Phase 1 primary objective:

  • Evaluate the safety and tolerability of INCB039110 in combination with ibrutinib in subjects with relapsed or refractory diffuse large B-cell lymphoma (DLBCL)

Phase 2 primary objective:

  • Evaluate efficacy of INCB039110 in combination with ibrutinib in subjects with R/R non-germinal center B-cell DLBCL as demonstrated by ORR

Phase 2 secondary objectives:

  • Evaluate efficacy in terms of DOR, durable response rate, PFS, and OS
  • Evaluate safety and tolerability of treatment combination





Study Design




Key inclusion criteria:

  • Age ≥18 years
  • R/R histologically document DLBCL with 1-3 prior treatment regimens
    • For phase 1, any DLBCL subtype
    • For phase 2, ABC or unclassifiable subtypes of DLBCL
  • Fluorodeoxyglucose-avid disease per the Lugano Classification1
  • Eastern Cooperative Oncology Group performance status 0 to 2

Key exclusion criteria:

  • Transformed DLBCL or DLBCL with coexistent histologies (eg, follicular or mucosa-associated lymphoid tissue lymphoma)
  • Primary mediastinal (thymic) large B-cell lymphoma
  • Known central nervous system lymphoma (either primary or metastatic)
  • Prior autologous or allo-HSCT, or active graft-versus-host-disease following allo-HSCT
  • Prior or concurrent therapy with a JAK inhibitor, Bruton’s tyrosine kinase inhibitor, or lenalidomide (phase 2 only)
