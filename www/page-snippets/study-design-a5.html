Phase 2 Study of the Safety, Tolerability, and Efficacy of INCB050465 (PI3Kδ Inhibitor)
in Combination With Ruxolitinib in Subjects With Myelofibrosis

KEY INCLUSION &
EXCLUSION CRITERIA

Primary objectives:

  • Part 1: Evaluate safety and tolerability of INCB050465 (PI3Kδ inhibitor) in combination with ruxolitinib in subjects with MF and select a dose for further evaluation
  • Part 2: Evaluate efficacy of the combination on spleen volume reduction

secondary objectives:

  • Evaluate efficacy of INCB050465 in combination with ruxolitinib on MF symptoms
  • Evaluate efficacy of combination on response using International Working Group–Myeloproliferative Neoplasms Research and Treatment criteria
  • Assess pharmacokinetics

Study Design

Key inclusion criteria:

  • Age ≥18 years
  • Diagnosis of primary MF, post–polycythemia vera MF, or post–essential thrombocythemia MF
  • Treated with ruxolitinib for ≥6 months with stable dose for ≥8 weeks
  • Palpable splenomegaly >10 cm below left subcostal margin or splenomegaly 5 to 10 cm below left subcostal margin and active symptoms of MF
  • Subjects with an Eastern Cooperative Oncology Group performance status of 0, 1, or 2

Key exclusion criteria:

  • Recent history of inadequate bone marrow reserve
  • Inadequate liver or renal function
  • Use of experimental drug therapy for MF or any other standard drug (eg, danazol, hydroxyurea) with the exception of ruxolitinib within 6 months of starting study (combination) therapy

MF = myelofibrosis, PI3Kδ = phosphatidylinositol-3 kinase delta
