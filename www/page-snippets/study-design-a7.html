Primary objective:

  • Evaluate efficacy

secondary objectives:

  • Evaluate efficacy in subjects with different molecular subgroups
  • Evaluate safety

Study Design

Select inclusion criteria:

  • Age ≥18 years
  • Histologically or cytologically confirmed cholangiocarcinoma with radiographically measurable/evaluable disease per RECIST v.1.1 and disease progression after ≥1 line of prior systemic therapy
  • Documented disease progression after at least 1 line of prior systemic therapy
  • Eastern Cooperative Oncology Group performance status 0 to 2

Select exclusion criteria:

  • Prior receipt of selective FGFR inhibitor
  • Untreated or progressive brain or central nervous system metastases
  • History and/or current evidence of ectopic mineralization/calcification
  • Current evidence of corneal disorder/keratopathy confirmed by ophthalmologic examination
